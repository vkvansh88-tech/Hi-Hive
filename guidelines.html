Community Guidelines

Keeping Hi-Hive safe, respectful, and buzzing with positivity.

Respectful Communication

Treat every member of the hive with kindness. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying are strictly prohibited.

No Spam or Promotions

Keep the buzz clean. Avoid repetitive messages, unauthorized ads, or phishing links in public or private chats.

Privacy & Security

Do not share personal info of others without consent. Protecting our users' data is our highest priority.

No Harmful Content

Nudity, graphic violence, and illegal content are not allowed. We maintain a professional and safe environment.

Account Responsibility

You are responsible for all activity on your account. Keep your login credentials secure and private.

Reporting & Moderation

Our team reviews reports 24/7. Use the report button if you encounter behavior that breaks our rules.

What to DO

  • Be helpful to new users
  • Report suspicious activity immediately
  • Use positive and inclusive language
  • Respect different opinions and cultures

What to AVOID

  • Impersonating Hi-Hive staff or others
  • Sharing malware or harmful scripts
  • Spamming emojis or large files
  • Inciting violence or discrimination

Our Enforcement System

01

Warning

First-time minor offenses receive an official warning via email.

02

Suspension

Repeated violations lead to a temporary 7-day account freeze.

03

Permanent Ban

Severe or continuous violations result in permanent removal.

Be Kind. Stay Safe. Enjoy Hi-Hive.
